An app called Flatdango shows you whatever movies the theater is showing on a given day.

The film that is shown The season's top trending movie is displayed when a user visits the website.

A user ought to be capable of:

View the current film schedule.
From a list of movies, select one to view the poster.
view the duration.
view the time of the show.
view the remaining tickets.
Purchase a movie ticket.
To view the film's title, runtime, showtime, and available tickets, click on the title of the film.

Purchasing a ticket is as simple as clicking the "buy" button.

When a film is sold out and customers are unable to purchase tickets, a sold out button will appear next to the title of the film in the movie list window.
